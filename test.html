Learning Tips

  • Practice daily, even for a short time.
  • Immerse yourself in English media (movies, music, podcasts).
  • Don't be afraid to make mistakes; they are part of learning.
  • Find a language partner to practice speaking.
  • Set clear, achievable goals for your learning.
